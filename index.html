Elysiayn represents the bubbling and quirky curiosity for new adventure that has been a theme throughout my life. It has become the medium in which I have been able to explore and create.
During my younger years, it was my pen-name when creating my own vast imaginative worlds inspired by fantasy and mythical novels such as Game of Thrones, Eragon, Harry Potter, Howl's Moving Castle, Gregor the Overlander, and various Greek/Roman/Egyptian mythology stories.
It became my persona when campaigning in Dungeons and Dragons throughout my years at the University of South Florida, as I pushed my boundaries to challenge myself both academically and personally.
True to tradition, Elysiayn is now my username when creating new and exciting projects while enrolled at UCF Coding Boot Camp on my journey to become a Full Stack Developer. While currently based in Orlando, I look forward to where the world of coding will take me.

My name is Wendy Lemus and I code as Elysiayn!
